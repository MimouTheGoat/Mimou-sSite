You can't get access to Microsoft Office Suite; however, there's a better open-source solution: LibreOffice.

Now, you can't access LibreOffice online. Since it's open-source, there's no central funding to maintain online servers (they don't make any money). However, there is a workaround.

If you have a Google account, it's pretty easy. You can just use Google's free services: Docs, Sheets, Slides, Gmail, Drive, Meet, Calendar...

You can also create a free Microsoft account which allows you to access the web version of Microsoft Office.

If you don't have a Google Account or a Microsoft Account, then follow the instructions there.
